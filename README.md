## Winter Veil Vale - Reindeer Mount guide!

This is a guide on how to obtain the [Winter Veil Reindeer](https://database.turtle-wow.org/?item=21044) mount.
### Click the blue texts! they are image and database links!

Start off by heading to the Winter Veil Vale by talking to [**Frosty**](<https://imgur.com/a/UHfzYrL>).

At the vale, find [Mulin Snowbeard](<https://i.imgur.com/Bq2vaf6.jpeg>) and complete the quests [Inquiring About Legend](<https://database.turtle-wow.org/?quest=40780%3E>) & [Further Inquiries About Legend](<https://database.turtle-wow.org/?quest=40781%3E>) (unlocks after the first one is completed). 
**(All the NPCs are located around the big campfire near the quest giver)**
  
When both pre-quests are done, you will now see two new quests called [The Legend Comes To Life!](<https://database.turtle-wow.org/?quest=40782%3E>) and [Life Comes To The Legend!](<https://database.turtle-wow.org/?quest=40783%3E>)

### Locations:
**[The Legend Comes To Life!](<https://database.turtle-wow.org/?quest=40782%3E)** 
* [Winter Patriarch ](<https://imgur.com/a/sJ0qw00>) Mulgore - 57, 94 (Head west along the road to get there)
* [Ancient of Winter](<https://imgur.com/a/NSF34dA>) Winterspring - 55, 11
* [Father Bear](<https://imgur.com/a/9E2eEBe>) Ashenvale - 30, 55

**[Life Comes To The Legend!](https://database.turtle-wow.org/?quest=40783)**
* [Great Spirit of Winter](<https://imgur.com/a/wy04L5F>) Dun Morogh - 30, 60
* [Greatfather Winter](<https://imgur.com/a/DZILahO>) Dun Morogh - 66, 14 [**(Horde Path)**](<https://youtu.be/J10NZXo6zm8>)
* [Great-father Winter](<https://imgur.com/a/9OSe5xD>) Winter Veil Vale - 55, 28

**Click the location names marked in blue for images**

Once you've completed all of the above quests, the [Winter Veil Reindeer](<https://database.turtle-wow.org/?item=21044%3E) will be given to you in a followup quest called [A Voucher Of A Lifetime!](<https://database.turtle-wow.org/?quest=40784%3E)
